        Resume Tips

        Expert advice to make your resume stand out

        Tailor Your Resume

        Customize your resume for each job application by highlighting relevant skills and experiences that match the job description.

        Use Action Words

        Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "achieved," "managed," "developed" to make your accomplishments more impactful.

        Quantify Results

        Include numbers and metrics to demonstrate your impact. "Increased sales by 30%" is more powerful than "increased sales."

        Keep It Clean

        Use a clean, professional format with consistent fonts, spacing, and bullet points. Avoid cluttered layouts and fancy graphics.
